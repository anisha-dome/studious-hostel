About Us

The Story of Studious Hostels

Studious Hostel is a dream seen and nurtured by a young and dynamic entrepreneur Mr. Nitin Shinde since March 2010. At a very young age he is fulfilling the duty of local guardian of Studious Hostel students since 2010. At Studious Hostel we strive hard to offer a safe and apt environment to the engineers of tomorrow; as one of our body members is an Engineer from Vishwakarma Group of Institutes.
